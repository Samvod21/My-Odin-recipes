Burger

Description:

A sanwich consistes of a roasted & finely chopped meat

Ingredients

  • Finely chopped meat
  • Buns
  • Slice tomatoes
  • Onion
  • Bread
  • Salad
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. cook the meat
  2. cut the bread to two equal halfs
  3. take the one half & put some salad & slice tomatoes
  4. put the roasted meat on it
  5. put the cheese & the rest vegitables
  6. put the other half of the bread on the top
  7. test & enjoy
